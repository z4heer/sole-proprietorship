# Comprehensive Platform Structure

## 1. Introduction
This document outlines the comprehensive platform structure for the Sole Proprietorship Management System.

## 2. Overview of Operations
The system will cover the following key operations:

### 2.1 Customer Management
- Tracking customer information
- Managing customer relationships

### 2.2 Product Inventory
- Managing product listings
- Inventory tracking and updates

### 2.3 Billing and Invoicing
- Generating invoices
- Tracking payments

### 2.4 Order Management
- Order processing
- Order tracking

### 2.5 Supplier Management
- Supplier information management
- Order placements to suppliers

### 2.6 Reporting and Analytics
- Sales reporting
- Inventory analysis

### 2.7 User Management
- Managing user roles and permissions
- User activity tracking

### 2.8 Financial Management
- Expense tracking
- Profit and loss statements

### 2.9 Customer Support
- Managing customer inquiries
- Tracking support tickets

### 2.10 Compliance Management
- Ensuring regulatory compliance
- Document management for compliance

## 3. System Architecture
The platform will be built using a microservices architecture to ensure scalability and maintainability.

## 4. Technology Stack
- **Frontend:** ReactJS
- **Backend:** Node.js with Express
- **Database:** MongoDB
- **Hosting:** AWS or similar service

## 5. Conclusion
This document serves as a preliminary framework for the sole proprietorship management system, outlining the necessary components and structural design.
