# Service Packages

## Package 1: Tech Setup Starter

### Best for
Small businesses starting to organize their digital operations.

### Includes
- Basic discovery session
- Tool and workflow review
- Folder and file structure setup
- Basic Google Drive or Microsoft 365 organization
- Spreadsheet/tracker starter setup
- Handover notes

### Main outcome
A cleaner and more usable business operating setup.

---

## Package 2: Workflow Optimization System

### Best for
Businesses facing confusion, manual work, repeated mistakes, or process delays.

### Includes
- Current workflow review
- Process gap identification
- Improvement plan
- tracker or reporting system setup
- checklist or SOP documentation
- review and refinement round

### Main outcome
Better process control, reduced manual confusion, and improved business flow.

---

## Package 3: Digital Presence Launch

### Best for
Small businesses that need a simple, professional online business presence.

### Includes
- Business information collection
- page/content planning
- business profile writing support
- simple showcase or service page setup
- final handover guidance

### Main outcome
A clear and professional digital presence for trust and visibility.
