# Pricing Model

## Pricing principle
Solvexa Solutions should price services based on problem complexity, scope clarity, delivery time, and business impact rather than random quoting.

## Recommended pricing structure

### 1. Fixed package pricing
Use for clearly defined services with standard deliverables.
Best for:
- small business tech setup
- workflow improvement packages
- starter digital presence support

### 2. Custom project pricing
Use when scope depends on business size, tools, or process complexity.
Best for:
- process redesign
- multi-tool integration
- special documentation or dashboard work

### 3. Monthly support retainer
Use after project handover when a client needs continued help.
Best for:
- update requests
- minor fixes
- process support
- monthly improvement work

## Early pricing approach
Start simple:
- Discovery call: free or low-cost qualifying call
- Small setup package: fixed fee
- Medium workflow project: custom quote
- Ongoing support: monthly retainer

## Pricing rules
- Never quote without understanding scope.
- Define what is included and excluded.
- Limit revisions in fixed-fee work.
- Charge extra for urgent delivery.
- Ask for an advance before starting work.

## Suggested payment terms
- 50% advance before work starts
- 50% before final handover or on completion milestone
- Monthly retainers billed in advance

## Scope protection
If the client asks for new items outside the agreed scope, move them into:
- add-on pricing
- a separate phase
- a revised proposal
