# Website Structure

## Website goal
Present Solvexa Solutions clearly, build trust, explain services, and convert visitors into enquiries.

## Recommended pages

### 1. Home
Purpose:
- Explain what Solvexa Solutions does
- Show value quickly
- Direct users to services or contact

Sections:
- Hero section
- Problem to solution section
- Core service highlights
- Why choose us
- Simple process
- testimonial or trust section
- contact CTA

### 2. About
Purpose:
- Explain the business background
- Build trust through founder-led delivery
- Show service philosophy and approach

Sections:
- Founder intro
- Mission
- Working style
- Who we help
- Why the business exists

### 3. Services
Purpose:
- Present service packages clearly
- Help visitors identify the right offer

Sections:
- Service overview
- Package 1
- Package 2
- Package 3
- Add-ons
- CTA for enquiry

### 4. Portfolio / Case Studies
Purpose:
- Show examples of problems solved
- Demonstrate practical capability

Sections:
- Selected work list
- Case study summaries
- before/after outcomes
- tools used
- CTA

### 5. Contact
Purpose:
- Make enquiry simple
- Capture leads directly

Sections:
- short intro
- contact form
- email/phone details
- expected response time

### 6. Optional later pages
- FAQ
- Blog / Insights
- Privacy Policy
- Terms of Service
