## Launch checklist

Before launch, confirm the domain, repository, homepage content, contact method, brand consistency, mobile view, links, SEO title, favicon, and privacy policy. After launch, test the site, share it with a few trusted people, fix issues fast, and then add your first case study. [hostinger](https://www.hostinger.com/tutorials/website-launch-checklist)

## Case-study format

Each case study should show the problem, goal, approach, deliverables, result, and tools used, because that is the clearest way to demonstrate service value. This gives Solvexa Solutions proof of capability instead of only a description of services. [qwilr](https://qwilr.com/templates/category/case-study-templates/)
