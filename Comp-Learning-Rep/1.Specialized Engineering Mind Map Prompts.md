# Advanced Specialized Engineering Mind Map Prompts

These prompts are **NOT for every technology every day**.

These are used when you want:

* deeper engineering maturity
* architect-level thinking
* production expertise
* enterprise decision-making
* reliability thinking
* performance analysis
* security analysis

Think of them as:

```text id="u7x4k2"
Advanced Engineering Intelligence Modules
```

Use them selectively for:

* Kafka
* Kubernetes
* Redis
* PostgreSQL
* AWS
* Spring Boot
* Microservices
* Security
* Cloud systems
* Distributed systems

# 1. BUSINESS IMPACT MIND MAP PROMPT

## Purpose

```text id="c2p7m5"
Business-aware engineering thinking
```

Helps understand:

* business value
* customer impact
* operational efficiency
* cost optimization
* enterprise scalability

---

## Prompt

```text id="x8v1q4"
Act as a chief solution architect and engineering leader.

Create a BUSINESS IMPACT mind map for [SKILL].

PURPOSE:
Help understand how [SKILL] impacts business scalability, customer experience, operational efficiency, engineering productivity, and enterprise growth.

Cover:

1. Business problems solved
2. Customer experience impact
3. Performance impact
4. Reliability impact
5. Scalability impact
6. Infrastructure cost impact
7. Engineering productivity impact
8. Operational efficiency impact
9. Team collaboration impact
10. Cloud cost implications
11. Maintenance cost implications
12. Revenue/business growth enablement
13. Enterprise adoption reasons
14. Real-world business examples
15. Risks and tradeoffs
16. ROI considerations
17. Executive/leadership perspective
18. Solution architect perspective

Focus on:
- business value
- operational practicality
- enterprise engineering
- architecture decision reasoning

Format:
- business-oriented hierarchy
- engineering-to-business relationship mapping
- enterprise-focused visualization
```
