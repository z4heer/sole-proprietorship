# 🔥 1. WHAT

## Software Industry Understanding

The software industry is an ecosystem where:

* businesses
* users
* developers
* infrastructure
* applications
* data

work together to solve problems using software systems.

---

## Main Goal of Software Industry

Build software that:

* solves business problems
* improves efficiency
* automates work
* scales to many users
* generates business value

---

## Examples

* Banking apps
* E-commerce platforms
* Cloud systems
* Hospital software
* Trading platforms
* Social media apps

---

# 🎤 13. 60-SECOND INTERVIEW EXPLANATION

> “The software industry builds digital systems that solve business and user problems through software applications.
> It involves multiple areas like frontend, backend, databases, DevOps, cloud infrastructure, testing, and security.
> The industry works through the SDLC process including requirement gathering, development, deployment, and monitoring.
> Modern software systems focus heavily on scalability, reliability, automation, and security.
> Overall, the software industry combines technology, engineering, and business goals to deliver scalable digital solutions.”
